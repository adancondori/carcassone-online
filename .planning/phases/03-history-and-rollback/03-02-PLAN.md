```python
def post_rollback(client, game_id, action_id):
    """Submit a rollback action. Returns the response."""
    return client.post(
        f"/games/{game_id}/rollback",
        data={"action_id": str(action_id)},
        headers={"HX-Request": "true"},
    )
```

**TestRollback class** (tests for UNDO-02, UNDO-04):

1. `test_rollback_returns_fragments` -- Score 2 actions, rollback to action 1. Verify response is 200, no DOCTYPE (is a fragment), and contains `hx-swap-oob`.

3. `test_rollback_marks_subsequent_actions_undone` -- Score 3 actions. Rollback to action 1. Verify the response history contains the `undone` class for actions 2 and 3. Action 1 should NOT have the `undone` class.

4. `test_rollback_shared_action` -- Score 10 to both players (action 1), then score 5 to Alice only (action 2). Rollback to action 1. Verify both players show 10 (action 1 stays active), and only action 2 is marked undone.

5. `test_rollback_nothing_when_last_action` -- Score 1 action, rollback to that action (no subsequent actions to undo). Verify 200 response with unchanged scores.
